Our Story

CoolTech HVAC Solutions was founded in 2005 by Lane Kiffin with the goal of bringing transparent, reliable heating and cooling service to the Nashville area. Having worked in the industry for years, Lane noticed a lack of customer focus and clear communication. He built CoolTech on the core principles of integrity and expertise.

Today, with a growing team of certified technicians led by David Lee and efficient operations managed by Sammy Alderman, we continue to serve thousands of homes and businesses. We believe that true comfort is not just about temperature—it's about the peace of mind that comes from knowing your home is in the hands of professionals who truly care.
